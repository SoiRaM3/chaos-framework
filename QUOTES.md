# Quotes — The Chaos Framework

> A selection of lines from the Chaos Series, the Stokknes Chaos Theory Compendium, standalone philosophical works, and related fiction.

## Reality

> Reality is not gone. It is mediated.

> Reality is not the world you see, but the structure that forces you to see it.

> Reality is the survival version of truth.

> Full truth is uninhabitable.

> You never experience the world directly. You experience the story your brain can afford to tell about it.

> Reality is not truth. It is coherence.

> Reality is a hallucination with consensus issues.

## Information

> You used to pray for rain. Now you pray for signal.

> The problem is not illusion. It is overload.

> You are not shown reality. You are shown interpretations competing for your nervous system.

> The modern cave does not imprison belief. It farms perception.

> Reaction is enough to make illusion real inside the body.

> Too much information does not create clarity. It creates instability.

> Confusion is not the problem. Fragmentation is.

> Exposure is not understanding. Awareness is not leverage.

> You do not drown in data. You drown in interpretation.

> The algorithm did not create the wound. It learned where the wound was.

## Civilization

> Civilization is entropy with a user interface.

> Civilization is not a masterpiece. It is a frightened animal wearing infrastructure.

> Civilization is intelligent in fragments. Stupid in aggregate.

> Civilization does not operate from confidence. It operates from fear with paperwork.

> Empires are anxiety with logistics.

> Collapse does not always announce itself. Sometimes it becomes customer service.

> The organism has become powerful. It has not become graceful.

> The system continues. It simply asks more of everyone while delivering less.

> Civilization is the animal that built the cage.

> It is also the animal capable of noticing the bars.

## Power

> Power is the invisible architecture of permission.

> The cage expands as it becomes invisible.

> Control becomes environment.

> If it is free, the cage is you.

## Faith
