# The Chaos Framework — Structural Overview

---

## 1. Premise

Human beings do not suffer only because reality is difficult.

They suffer because:
- The systems interpreting reality collapse faster than meaning can stabilize.

---

## 2. Core Components

The framework operates across interacting domains:

- Reality  
- Time  
- Identity  
- Information  
- Power  
- Civilization  
- Faith  
- Entropy  

These are not separate topics.

They are pressure systems influencing each other.

---

## 3. The Three Reality Model

### Observable Reality
- Sensory input
- Lived experience
- Biased perception

### Measurable Reality
- Data systems
- Metrics
- Institutional reporting

### Constructed Reality
- Narratives
- Identity
- Belief systems

---

## 4. Instability Condition

Chaos emerges when:

- Observable ≠ Measurable  
- Measurable ≠ Constructed  
- Constructed dominates behavior  

Result:
- Cognitive fragmentation
- Perceptual instability
- Meaning collapse
